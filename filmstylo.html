Film Stylo

This is my submission for the 2024 Babel Film Workshop's activity called 'Film Stylo,' where participants were tasked with creating a short film centered around the theme of human touch. The rules of the competition were clear: the film had to be between 1-3 minutes in duration, and each filmmaker could only take on the roles of writer, director, cinematographer, and editor. Our interpretation of human touch served as the foundation for our storytelling. The film can be either voiceover-driven or subtitle-driven, with English subtitles provided if other languages are spoken. Additionally, the filmmaker's face was restricted to appear in just one shot. The film had to be appropriate for a school setting, and screen recording was not allowed. With these rules, I made the movie seen to the right.
